About Us

Technology allowed us to virtually travel to any location in the world. Ever since the remote work became the industry standard of how people work. The computer became a thing that you want to stay away from despite it’s practicality and it’s benefits. The purpose of Néo-Noise is to give the user an immersive auditorial experience of New York City. Néo-Noise by Néo-Manhattan is a web application that offers an immersive auditorial experience of New York City. The website will be updated regularly to introduce different areas in NYC by using sounds captured in the specific area we are trying to replicate. Inspired by IMissMyCafe, IMissMyBar, IMissTheOffice, and Coffitivity. All sounds were recorded and designed by Néo-Manhattan. Code by André Jeon and Namhyuk Woo. André Jeon is a New York based creative who is currently working on something bussin bussin. Namhyuk Woo is a California based Software developer currently working at SHEEEEEEESH. Illustration by Nam’s girlfriend. Follow her Instagram for her other works.
