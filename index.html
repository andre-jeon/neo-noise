About Us

The purpose of Néo-Noise is to give the user an immersive auditorial experience of New York City. We wanted to create a website that would be able to capture the bustling city in its full glory without leaving the safety of your home.

Made by the creators of Néo-Manhattan, this web application offers a way for you to personalize every sound to your tastes. Feel free to experiment with the audio levels of each sound to find the perfect urban symphony for you. The website will be updated regularly to introduce different areas in NYC by using sounds captured in the specific area we are trying to replicate.
